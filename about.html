About School

Located at Garnet Valley Middle School, PA.

Delaware Chester Community School (DCCS) is a non-profit school, located in Garnet Valley school district in the south-western suburb of Philadelphia. It was founded in Summer 2020. The purpose of the School is to teach Chinese language, STEM arts and sports classes, to K-12 students in local communities to promote the awareness of the Chinese culture and heritage, as well as to facilitate the mutual understanding between Chinese and other ethnic groups in the local community through educational and cultural activities and events.

What We Offer

We are qualified & of experience in this field.

Our academic courses are taught by teachers with Master's or Ph.D degrees with years' of teaching experience. Available courses include academic subjects, dance & art, and more, for both children and adults. We also provide a variety of exciting indoor and outdoor sports activities. With typically 5-10 and a maximum of 12 students in a class, teachers are able to give great attention to each and every student.

Our Tenets

Having Fun While Learning, Together!

Fun has a positive effect on motivation levels, determining what we learn and how much we retain. Learning isn’t a one-off event. It requires repetition and dedication. If the experience is fun, learners will stay curious and keep coming back for more.

Our Story

Delaware Chester Community School is a non-profit enrichment school founded in 2020 by parents in the Garnet Valley area in the southwest Philadelphia Suburb. The school is dedicated to providing additional academic courses and extracurricular classes for our children from Kindergarten through Grade 12.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire our children to succeed in school, sports, and life. We challenge our K-12 students to realize their potential not only by offering subject-specific classes, such as English and Math, that are taught at schools, but also extending beyond with courses such as foreign languages, coding, public speaking, music, art, and sports.
