About School

Located at Garnet Valley Middle School, PA.

Delaware Chester Community School (DCCS) is a non-profit educational institution located in the Garnet Valley School District, in the southwestern suburbs of Philadelphia. Founded in the summer of 2020, DCCS is dedicated to offering high-quality, affordable enrichment education in Chinese language, STEM, arts, and sports to K-12 students beyond regular school hours. Our mission is to cultivate an appreciation for Chinese culture and heritage while fostering mutual understanding and respect among diverse ethnic groups. Through a variety of educational programs and cultural events, we strive to build a vibrant, inclusive community that celebrates cultural diversity and promotes lifelong learning.

What We Believe

Learning for Life.

We offer a wide range of courses and activities for both children and parents. For K-12 students, we provide academic subject classes such as English and Math, and extend our curriculum to include foreign languages, coding, public speaking, music, dance, art, and sports. For parents, we offer enriching classes and activities, including arts and sports programs. Additionally, we have sports clubs where parents can participate at a very low cost, fostering community engagement and promoting a healthy lifestyle.

What We Offer

We are qualified & of experience in this field.

Our dedicated team of educators includes teachers with master's and Ph.D. degrees, as well as professors from universities, and renowned local argist that teaches art classes. We also have high school students who primarily teach math classes to younger students or lead clubs such as chess and debate. We are meticulous in selecting our teachers, striving to provide an environment that offers quality education to students and a platform for high schoolers to volunteer and contribute to the community by teaching children. With typically 5-10 and a maximum of 12 students per class, our teachers can give great attention to each and every student.

Our Tenets

Having Fun While Learning, Together!

Fun has a positive effect on motivation levels, determining what we learn and how much we retain. Learning isn’t a one-off event. It requires repetition and dedication. If the experience is fun, learners will stay curious and keep coming back for more.

Our Story

Delaware Chester Community School is a non-profit enrichment school founded in 2020 by parents in the Garnet Valley area in the southwest Philadelphia Suburb. The school is dedicated to providing additional academic courses and extracurricular classes for our children from Kindergarten through Grade 12.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire our children to succeed in school, sports, and life. We challenge our K-12 students to realize their potential not only by offering subject-specific classes, such as English and Math, that are taught at schools, but also extending beyond with courses such as foreign languages, coding, public speaking, music, art, and sports.
