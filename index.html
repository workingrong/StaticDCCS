    Python Coding Classes Open for Registration!

    The Delaware Steam Academy provides our coding classes. Tuitions are lowered to $220 from $259 for DCCS students for ten lessons, 90 minutes per lesson.

    Class Introductions


    Python Level 1 (10 - 14 years old)
    Python Level 2 (10 - 14 years old)
    Python Level 3 (11 - 15 years old)
    Register

    Python Level 1

    ×
    In this course, students will learn some Python fundamentals, how to build simple Python based games using programming loops and functions, and how to draw graphics using the Python module Turtle. Be able to use the in-built Python modules for their own projects.

    Python Level 2

    ×
    Students will learn both fundamental programming concepts and Python project examples. Top students will be invited to participate in the American Computer Science League.

    Python Level 3

    ×
    In this online live course, students will learn the most popular programming language for software engineering, data science, machine learning and AI. This program is for intermediate/advanced students who have at least 10 hours of Python experience.

    Our Organization

    Provide best education for and by community.

    Delaware Chester Community School is a non-profit enrichment school founded in 2020 by parents in the Garnet Valley area in the southwest Philadelphia Suburb. The school is dedicated to providing additional academic courses and extracurricular classes for our children from Kindergarten through Grade 12.

    Rising Young Stars

    We are bound by the understanding that our students should take an active part in both school and communities. Therefore, for high schoolers interested in teaching a subject, we also provide them opportunities to design and start a class. This is a way to prepare them better for college and career and also to serve our local communities actively and responsibly. Please don't hesitate to get in touch with us, should you have any questions.

    Community Involvement

    Excellence in education requires the participation of our parents. There is much to be done and extra hands are always needed and appreciated. Teens are welcome to join us in the fulfillment of their community service hours! If you seek to enhance your children's education, and want to get more involvement, please join us to build a warm and caring community where our kids can thrive. Please don't hesitate to get in touch with us, if you want to know more.

    Employment Opportunities

    Delaware Chester Community School is seeking qualified teachers for drawing, dancing, coding, and languages etc. Teachers would be responsible for on site in-person instruction, and virtual instruction as needed, once a week. If you are interested, please send us your resume and cover letter by email to info@dccs-us.org.
